Dream Diaries: A Dream Podcast

PURPOSE

I am fascinated by sleep and dreaming, and I had always wanted to keep a journal of all my dreams to interpret, but I could never devote 15 minutes in the morning to immediately jot down my dreams. Plus, my handwriting is so sloppy before I complete my morning routine.

One day, my friend suggested I record myself. An excellent idea!

After playing back my 1st voice recorded dream entry, I thought it would be fun to share it with the rest of the world through a podcast. I designed the podcast, gave it a name, determined the demographic, and now I host a podcast!

You can listen to the show here.

PROCESS

ARTWORK

I wanted to showcase a girl sleeping a night while keeping the idea of high contrast. At first, the girl’s hair was dark purple with the dark blue background, but I recently updated the artwork to reature a girl with pink hair to help her stand out.

NAME CONCEPTION

I brainstormed a list of names that popped into my head. I landed at Dream Diaries because the alliteration hit the sweet spot. I looked online for any media names that were similar to mine. To my dismay, there was already a podcast called “Dream Diaries”, so I added “A Dreamcast” to the end of my title to help differentiate. I also believe that my artwork adds to its uniqueness.

EDITING AUDIO

I edited my first episode in Audacity because I primarily focused on cutting out silent portions of my recording. I realized shortly after that I owned Adobe Premiere Pro, a more powerful software system. Although you would normally use it to edit video, it has awesome integrations for editing audio (audio is in videos, after all :D). I researched best ways to edit audio. I’m still in the process of figuring it all out, but I’m having fun all the while.

UPLOADING TO A PODCAST

Uploading is not as simple as one may think! I initially thought it would be similar to uploading onto a website like Youtube, but there is an extra step beforehand. You must upload the audio to a podcast hosting website first, then list it in directories such as Apple Podcasts or Spotify. The process was intuitive, and before I knew it, I had a few episodes going on Apple Podcasts.

RESULT

So far, I’ve been sharing my page to family and friends who express interest in the show. It is an interesting concept, as the content creation is contingent upon my remembering my dreams, which is not guaranteed. Despite that, I have learned so much about audio editing and podcast hosting that I would do it again!
